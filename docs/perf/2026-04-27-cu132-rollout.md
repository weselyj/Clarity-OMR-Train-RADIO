## Bench results

### Phase 3 — cu132 baseline (350 opt-steps Stage 2, no compile)

Source: `bench/cu132_700/profile.jsonl`, `scripts/profile_summary.py --warm 100` (250 measured opt-steps after 100 warm). Run on `venv-cu132` (torch 2.13.0.dev20260426+cu132, cuDNN 9.21.01) with `--diag-cadence 1` for parity with the cu128 reference methodology.

| Phase | Mean ms | % of wall | cu128 ref | Δ |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| gpu_backward | 3097 | 59.8% | 3255 (60.7%) | **−4.9%** |
| gpu_forward | 1748 | 33.7% | 1828 (34.1%) | **−4.4%** |
| gpu_grad_diagnostics | 118 | 2.3% | 119 (2.2%) | — |
| cpu_encode | 106 | 2.1% | 48 (0.9%) | **+121%** ⚠ |
| cpu_augment | 75 | 1.5% | 76 (1.4%) | — |
| cpu_h2d | 5 | 0.1% | 5 (0.1%) | — |
| gpu_optimizer | 5 | 0.1% | 5 (0.1%) | — |
| cpu_log_io | 0.15 | 0.0% | 0.2 (0.0%) | — |
| cpu_sample | 0.30 | 0.0% | 0.3 (0.0%) | — |
| **Wall mean** | **5180** | — | 5362 | **−3.4%** |

Phase coverage 99.5%; residual ~25 ms/step is uninstrumented overhead.

**cpu_encode regression note:** cu132 venv has pillow 12.2.0; cu128 venv had 12.1.1. The cpu_encode phase is dominated by image decode/resize via PIL. Worth a small follow-up (downgrade pillow or profile encode internals), but encoder wins more than swallow it on this workload.

`cpu_pipeline_pct` = (75 + 106 + 5 + 0.15 + 0.30) / 5180 = **3.6%** — informs Phase 6 decision below.
